VietLIS PROJECT

A package project contains 2 product systems: VietLIS (Development and construction of cadastral and land information management system) and LandValuation (Land price information management system development and construction)

Overview

In the context of rapid urbanization in Vietnam, the Government decided to upgrade the system which mange land / house in Vietnam into advance and modern technology of GIS system of Korea who are using it in their landing management. The LIS management integrated Enterprise GIS products (GeoNURIS) that can handle from data layers to applications layers.
The project aims to develop a package of landing information system which various users: expert , citizens, governors with optimize for web-base system, that can manage, process and analyze spatial data. The system contain a web GIS engine the provides information required by the user according to geospatial information web service interface standard defined by the OGC international standard.

Visual design

My task was to take over the design from the previous VietLIS system which was more or less just basic html/css without styling and came up with consistent branding as well as design guidelines. After that, I had to apply the new style of design for the whole package of project (VietLIS and Landvaluation)

The system was reponsive on digital devices which was mainly used: Desktop, Notebook, Tablet and secondary focus on Mobile.

With the insights gained from Study about professional of landing system and the old landing system, I documented the different iterations of the design system


Outcomes

Always consider everyone who is working/previously worked on the project. Since we picked up a lot of work that was dropped from a defunct team, I learned that I have to consider the needs of everyone that was involved in the project. Even though I had plenty of ideas, I had to sometimes step back and consider if they were realistic with the development team’s time and the restraints on code bases that we were migrating out of or built from scratch.
